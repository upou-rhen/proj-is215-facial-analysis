HOW TO USE?

Upload Your Photo


Start by uploading a photo of your choice using the provided upload form. The photo can be of anything - a landscape, a group of people, an object, or anything else you'd like to explore further.


Photo Analysis


Once you've uploaded your photo, our system will analyze it using advanced algorithms to identify objects, scenes, and other elements within the image.



Generated Article


After analysis, we'll generate a news-like article summarizing the content of your photo. This article will provide descriptions, insights, and interesting facts about the objects and scenes detected in your image.

Explore the Results


You'll then be redirected to a page where you can explore the generated article and learn more about what's in your photo.
