about

We are a dynamic group of students driven by a shared passion for technology and innovation. In this project, we have pooled our diverse talents and expertise to develop a groundbreaking application that showcases our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.

Our application harnesses the power of Amazon Web Services (AWS), leveraging services such as AWS S3 Bucket, AWS Lambda Function, and Amazon Rekognition. This powerful combination allows us to analyze images with unparalleled accuracy and efficiency.

Upon receiving an image, our application initiates a series of automated processes. The image is uploaded to an AWS S3 Bucket, triggering an AWS Lambda Function to analyze the faces detected within the image using Amazon Rekognition. The output is a detailed JSON file outlining facial features and characteristics.

But we didn't stop there. We wanted to add a personal touch to our project. That's why we integrated ChatGPT, an advanced natural language processing model, to dynamically generate a news article about the individual depicted in the image. This adds a unique and engaging element to the user experience.

As MIS students, we understand the importance of learning and growth. This project has been an invaluable learning experience for each member of our team, allowing us to expand our knowledge and skills in areas such as cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and natural language processing.
